About Dice Counter

Dice Count is the ultimate tool for tabletop gamers. Whether you're playing board games, role-playing games, or just need to keep track of dice rolls, Dice Counter has you covered.

When playing games you can track the rolled dice and see the averages in your current game compared to the overall average. Are you always unlucky in dice games? But do your friends/family never believe you? Prove it by tracking the dice counts and seeing the averages.

Features

  • Easy-to-use interface
  • Count and record dice rolls
  • Insights and averages
